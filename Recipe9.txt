\f0\b\fs24 \cf0 Chorizo stuffed bell peppers: https://www.budgetbytes.com/chorizo-stuffed-bell-peppers/\
3 bell peppers\
1 lb. Mexican chorizo (not the cured Spanish kind)\
1 yellow onion \
2 cloves garlic\
1 4oz can diced green chiles\
2 Tbsp tomato paste\
2 tsp chili powder\
1/2 tsp cumin\
1/2 tsp garlic powder\
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper\
1/4 tsp salt\
1/2 cup long grain white rice (uncooked)\
1 cup chicken broth \
1 cup shredded mozzarella\
\
Preheat oven to 350. Core out peppers, put in baking dish cut side up and bake 15 mins. Cook chorizo then add onion and garlic and cook until softened. Add diced chiles, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and salt to skillet. Add rice and chicken broth, stir. Cover and bring to boil then lower heat and simmer for 20 mins. Let rest for 5 min before fluffing then add to bell peppers. Top with shredded cheese and bake for 20 mins. \
